Czech Republic for OpenGuessr

  • position

    • Czech Republic is in Central Europe (The heart of Europe)

  • Czechia borders with:

    • GERMANY SLOVAKIA POLAND AUSTRIA

  • Typical geography

    • most of the country = hills and uplands lots of forests fields alternating with villages no sea mountains mainly along the borders

  • Climate

    • four seasons in summer the landscape is green in winter often gray and foggy snow mainly in the mountains

    Road

  • Directional signs

    • blue directional signs white town signs with black text yellow detours

  • Road numbers

    • D1 = highway I/43 = main road II/xxx and III/xxx = smaller

  • Highway signs:

    • red number in a white field

  • Curb stones

    • white black top orange reflectors

    language

  • The best indicator:

    • ř č š ž ě ů When you see 'ř', it's almost certainly Czech.

  • Typical words
  • On the signs:

    • obec-municipality centrum- centre náměstí- square pozor- attention průjezd- passage zákaz- prohibition vlakové nádraží- train station

  • In the cities:

    • potraviny- groceries hospoda- tavern restaurace- restaurant

  • Traffic:

    • zastávka- bus stop jednosměrka- one-way street objížďka- detour

    Vesnice-Village

  • Houses

    • colorful facades low houses red roofs plaster instead of wood

  • Village green

    • Almost every village has: a small chapel or church a bus stop a pub a pond

  • Roads in villages

    • narrow without sidewalks houses close to the road
